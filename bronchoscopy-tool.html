Clinical Considerations

Equipment Compatibility: Always verify that the telescope fits through the selected bronchoscope before the procedure.

Ventilation: Ventilating bronchoscopes allow for continuous oxygenation during the procedure.

Optical Forceps: Size selection depends on the bronchoscope diameter and the pathology being addressed.

Safety: These are guidelines only. Clinical judgment and patient-specific factors should always take precedence.

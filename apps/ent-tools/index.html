Built for daily ENT utility and reference

ENT Hub is designed to keep common otolaryngology utility/reference workflows close at hand while preserving the clinical responsibility of the user.

Airway references

Structured airway, bronchoscopy, endotracheal tube, and tracheostomy sizing references for quick educational lookup.

Scoring and reference tools

Non-drug ENT scoring and reference workflows for otology, rhinology, laryngology, airway, sleep, and general use.

Flashcards and study

Study decks, tagged cards, progress tracking, and review tools for structured learning across ENT topics.

Case logs

Personal case logging with clear reminders not to enter patient identifiers or protected health information.

Patient instructions

Admin-published educational handouts and instructions for organized reference, with privacy-aware support guidance.

Announcements

App announcements and updates for courses, conferences, educational content, and project communication.

Safety and App Store positioning

ENT Hub is positioned as utility/reference software for healthcare professionals. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, mitigate, prescribe, or prevent disease, and it does not replace clinical judgment, institutional policy, pharmacist review, specialist consultation, or emergency care.

  • Clear utility/reference positioning with no diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, or patient-specific advice claims.
  • Users are warned not to enter patient names, medical record numbers, national IDs, phone numbers, dates of birth, or other protected identifiers.
  • Privacy and support pages are available publicly for App Review and users.

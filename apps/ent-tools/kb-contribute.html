Contribution rules

No patient identifiers Do not include names, dates, photos, MRNs, contact details, or any case-specific private data.
2
Source-backed writing Add references for clinical statements, guidelines, thresholds, and management points.
3
Durable accepted content Approved contributions may remain as app educational content after account deletion.
4
Public profile credit Contributor credit uses the public profile name. Deleted accounts lose personal attribution.

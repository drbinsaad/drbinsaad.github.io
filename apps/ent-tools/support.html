ENT Hub Support

For help with ENT Hub, contact drbinsaad@gmail.com.

Before You Contact Support

  • Do not send patient names, medical record numbers, national IDs, phone numbers, dates of birth, images containing identifiers, or other protected health information.
  • Include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
  • If the issue is about sign-in or account deletion, mention the error message without sharing your password.

Medical Disclaimer

ENT Hub is educational/reference software for healthcare professionals. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, mitigate, or prevent disease. It does not replace clinical judgment, institutional protocols, pharmacist review, or specialist consultation.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account in the app. A recent sign-in may be required before account deletion for security.

Cotton-Myer grades

Grade I
0–50%
Mild stenosis — usually well tolerated.
Grade II
51–70%
Moderate stenosis — may cause respiratory symptoms.
Grade III
71–99%
Severe stenosis — significant airway compromise.
Grade IV
No lumen
Complete obstruction — critical airway emergency.

Laryngology & Voice

Voice and laryngopharyngeal reflux assessment instruments

Voice Handicap Index (VHI)

Validated voice quality-of-life assessment with VHI-10 (0-40 scale) and VHI-30 (0-120 scale) versions covering functional, physical, and emotional voice impact domains.

Use Calculator

Reflux Symptom Index (RSI)

9-item laryngopharyngeal reflux screening tool (0-45 scale). Score >13 suggests LPR. Includes hoarseness, throat clearing, dysphagia, and heartburn assessment.

Use Calculator

Sleep Medicine

Obstructive sleep apnea screening and airway assessment tools

Epworth Sleepiness Scale (ESS)

8-item validated questionnaire (0-24 scale) for excessive daytime sleepiness screening. Score >10 indicates abnormal sleepiness; >16 suggests severe sleep disorder.

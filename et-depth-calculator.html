For infants 1-4 kg: Depth (cm) = 6 + Weight (kg)

Clinical Considerations

Verification Required: Always confirm ET tube placement with auscultation, chest rise, capnography, and chest X-ray

Tube Position: Ideal position is 2-3 cm above the carina (T2-T4 vertebral level)

Adjustments: May need adjustment based on patient anatomy, neck position, and clinical assessment

Nasal Intubation: Add 2-3 cm to calculated depth for nasotracheal intubation

Limitations: Formulas provide estimates only. Individual patient factors may require depth adjustments
